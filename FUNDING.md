# CeloHT Funding Policy

## Principles

- **Transparency:** Public reporting of all funds.
- **Accountability:** Community oversight ensures proper use.
- **Mission Alignment:** Funds support Education, Agents, or Reforestation.
- **Non-Custodial Preference:** No direct custody of user funds.
- **Zero Speculation:** Platform is not an investment or trading vehicle.

## Accepted Funding Sources

- Grants and donations from individuals, institutions, or organizations supporting the pillars.
- Sponsorships aligned with CeloHT mission.
- Minimal transaction fees strictly reinvested into initiatives.

## Utilization

All accepted funds are **allocated to programs** that directly impact the three pillars:

- **Education:** Learning materials, workshops, and digital resources.
- **Agents:** Incentives, operational costs, and community engagement.
- **Reforestation:** Tree planting, ecological programs, and sustainability tools.
