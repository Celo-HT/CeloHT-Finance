# CeloHT Tokenomics & Finance Report

## Overview

CeloHT is **not a cryptocurrency or token**. It is a decentralized platform designed to **support the Celo ecosystem**, using **Celo cUSD** through **Valora** for transactions, educational programs, community agent activities, and reforestation initiatives.

### Three Pillars

1. **Education** – Training and learning resources in Web3, blockchain, and financial literacy.
2. **Agents** – Local agents facilitate transactions and community adoption of cUSD.
3. **Reforestation** – Environmental impact initiatives funded by platform activities.



## Financial Principles

- **Non-Custodial Transactions:** All transactions occur via **Celo cUSD & Valora** wallets.
- **Transparency:** All inflows and outflows are publicly documented.
- **Mission-Aligned Funding:** Funds are strictly allocated to pillar activities.
- **No Speculation:** CeloHT does not issue tokens or investment products.



## Funding & Utilization

### Sources

- Grants and donations
- Sponsorships aligned with pillars
- Transaction fees via Valora (reinvested into pillars)

### Allocation

| Pillar        | Purpose                                               |
|---------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| Education     | Workshops, training programs, digital resources     |
| Agents        | Agent incentives, operational support, community events |
| Reforestation | Tree planting, sustainability projects               |



## Key Notes

- **No native token or cryptocurrency** is issued by CeloHT.  
- All operations leverage **existing Celo ecosystem assets**.  
- Platform governance is **community-driven** and decentralized.


## Conclusion

CeloHT is a **platform and ecosystem facilitator**, not a cryptocurrency. Its financial model is **transparent, mission-driven, and aligned with the three pillars**.
